IV Drip Treatments

A type of intravenous therapy that involves the administration of a combination of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants directly into the bloodstream through a needle or catheter.

Improved Skin Health

Hydration

Enhanced Skin Brightening

Detoxification

Customization

 

PROCEDURE APPROX. 30 MINUTES

Types of Drip

HANGOVER

Helps to alleviate the symptoms of a hangover after excessive alcohol consumption.

 

PRE-PARTY PRE-PARTY

Aims to hydrate the body, boost energy levels, and provide nutrients that can potentially help reduce the severity of a hangover the next day.

 

BEAUTY INSIDE OUT

(Whitening Drip) Beauty IV drips focus on enhancing skin health and complexion from within, promoting a more even skin tone, and improving skin radiance.

 

IRON INFUSION

Restores and boosts iron levels in the body quickly.

 

POWER SLEEP POWER SLEEP

To improve sleep quality and duration, with the help of nutrients like magnesium and Vitamin D3, which are known for their calming and sleep-inducing effects.

 

MYERS ENERGY BOOSTER

Formulated with vitamin C, B-complex vitamins, gluthathione, alpha lipoic acid, and magnesium, which enhance energy levels, combat fatigue, and support overall health.

 

SUPER NAD+

NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a coenzyme that plays a crucial role in cellular energy production. Super NAD+ IV drips aim to increase NAD+ levels in the body, potentially providing a boost in energy and supporting cellular health.
