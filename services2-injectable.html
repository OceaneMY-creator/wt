Way Injectable Treatments

Medical procedures where medications or substances are administered into the body through a needle or syringe.
 

Types of Treatment

BOTULINUM TOXIN

Botulinum toxin known as Botox is commonly used for cosmetic purposes to reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines on the face. It can also be used for certain medical conditions, such as muscle spasms and excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis).

Examples:


Wrinkle reduction

Cheek augmentation

Nasolabial fold reduction

Tear trough treatment

Forehead enhancement
