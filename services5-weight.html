WEIGHT LOSS WITH LIPOLYSIS

Weight loss with lipolysis is a non-surgical cosmetic procedure that aims to reduce localized fat deposits in specific areas of the body. Lipolysis involves the use of injectables to break down and dissolve fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by the body's lymphatic system.
