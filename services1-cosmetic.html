Cosmetic Grade Peels

Helps to improve the appearance and texture of the skin.

Types of Treatment

ACNE-A-WAY


This peel renews, brightens and smooths the skin while providing antibacterial support and reduce scarring from skin blemishes.

WAY AGELESS PEEL


It is known as the retinoic-acid peel, and works to reduce the appearance of visible signs of aging and sun damage.

WAY GLOWING PEEL


It can help improve overall skin tone and texture to smooth and brighten skin.

WAY SPOTLESS PEEL


It gently reduces the appearance of pigmentation and helps prevent future skin discoloration.

BACNE-A-WAY


It helps to reduce the appearance of scars and dark marks, but it also eliminates the risk of acne in the future.

Fun Fact

In ancient times, cosmetic peels were also used to improve the appearance of scars and wounds.
