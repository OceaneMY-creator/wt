HOW
Does Fotona 4D Work?

STEP 1: SMOOTHLIFTIN™


Starts inside the mouth, where laser energy is used to immediately kick start the tightening process around the jawline and perioral region.


Reduce expression lines like the nasolabial folds and wrinkles


STEP 2: FRAC3®


To complement the intraoral treatment, deeper imperfections (like deep lines) and pigments are precisely targeted.


Restore a youthful texture and more uniform overall complexion.


STEP 3: PIANO®


A brushing technique is used to safely and rapidly apply heating to create an overall tightening effect.


Resulting in more firm and lifted skin


STEP 4: SUPERFICIAL™


A light laser peel is done to improve the appearance of the skin and address surface-level imperfections.
